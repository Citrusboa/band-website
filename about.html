Coldplay

ABOUT

A band formed in 1996 and remains one of the best bands in the world. they created many timeless songs and won multiple awards. they are one of the most loved bands in the history of music-making. Their top songs include Viva La Vida, The Scientist, Strawberry Swing, Yellow, and the newest hit in collaboration with BTS: Universe. The band members are incredibly talented in ways unimaginable and their lyrics are beautifully crafted.
